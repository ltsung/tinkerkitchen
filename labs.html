Tinker Kitchen Labs

Love food hacking? So do we! Tinker Kitchen Labs is an opportunity to meet every week and deeply explore a cooking topic by making cool stuff to eat.

Details and Pricing

Labs are small group cooking meet-ups for informally trying out new recipes and techniques. We meet on Thursday evenings.

The cost is $20 per month plus materials for the labs you attend. Materials costs vary (truffles more than ramen noodles, for example), but average around $10-15.

Tinker Kitchen does all the organizing and shopping prior to the Lab. We eat what we make, assuming our lab experiments turn out okay. Otherwise, we can always order pizza as backup :)

Lab Topics

We discuss possible topics ahead of time and do a quick survey to decide what to make.

Check out our photo stream on instagram to see many of our yummy Labs creations. Here are a few things we’ve made recently:

  • Ramen noodles (from scratch!)
  • Modernist mac & cheese
  • Pressure-cooker paella with sous-vide seafood (photo).
