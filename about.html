About Tinker Kitchen


Tinker Kitchen is a community of food hackers who love to experiment with cooking.

We’re building a maker space with all the cooking gadgets you can’t fit into your kitchen. You can play with industrial culinary hardware like sous vide circulators, commercial pasta extruders, chocolate tempering machines, and more!

Workshops

Our workshops are your opportunity to play with some amazing kitchen toys, learn new cooking techniques, and have some good fun while at it.

Click the photos to see more, or check out our Instagram page.

You can also see our upcoming workshops, or learn about our private workshops we can customize for your location.
