Healthy cooking workshop

Start the New Year with a Healthy Tinker Kitchen Workshop! We'll use a variety of specialized equipment in our kitchen to make a healthy menu consisting of:

  • Salmon and asparagus using sous vide equipment
  • Fresh spinach pasta using a commercial pasta extruder
  • Fruit sorbet using an ice cream batch freezer

Features: Anova and Sansaire sous vide circulators, Bottene Lillo Due pasta extruder, Lello Musso Pola ice cream batch freezer.

Sous vide workshop

A perfect crash course on sous vide cooking. Learn how to effectively and safely use this technique to make a variety of dishes, including eggs, fish, beef, and chicken. Control temperature and time to achieve the effects you want. Bonus: sear with a Searzall-equipped torch.

Features: Anova and Sansaire sous vide circulators, Searzall + Bernzomatic TS-8000 torch.

Chocolate workshop

Chocolate making is great fun! Learn about the chocolate tempering process and what gives chocolate its shiny texture and snap. Make molded chocolates with beautiful designs and ganache filling, make dipped fruit and more. At the end, you'll take some chocolates home to share with friends.

Features: Chocovision Revolation V chocolate tempering machine, molds, transfer sheets.

Coffee workshop

Learn how to roast your own coffee, grind and brew espresso, and the basics of latte art. Bonus: we'll also grind spices and make delicious chai tea from scratch.

Features: FreshRoast SR500 air roaster, Rocket R58 espresso machine.

Ice cream workshop

An in-depth exploration of ice cream making, using commercial-grade equipment and sous vide techniques to create custard-base ice cream. Understand the science behind ice cream's taste and texture, and how to craft your own ice cream flavors.

Features: Anova and Sansaire sous vide circulators, Lello Musso Pola ice cream batch freezer.

Fresh pasta workshop

Learn pasta extrusion techniques using an old-fashioned manual as well as modern commercial equipment. Make short and long pasta, filled ravioli, and easy everyday sauces you can make at home.

Features: Torchio and Bottene Lillo Due pasta extruders with bronze and teflon pasta dies for campanelle, linguine, spaghetti, rigatoni, etc.

Details and Pricing

Cost is $65/person and include all materials and equipment you will need.

Workshops are about 3 hours and limited to small groups. Larger events are possible with a custom format, contact us to find out more.

Location is flexible – Tinker Kitchen can come to your space, with minimal requirements (counter space, sink, fridge). We bring all the materials and specialized equipment. Or, you can come to the Tinker Kitchen Lab in Mountain View, CA. Check out our photo album to get an idea of the space.
